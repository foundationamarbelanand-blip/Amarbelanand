Visionary Founder – Mahatma Amarbelanand Ji

Mahatma Amarbelanand Ji, the visionary founder of the Amarbelanand Foundation, was born in Mumbai, Maharashtra, and pursued his Bachelor of Science from Mumbai University. Gifted with an exceptionally sharp intellect, he initially aspired to pursue MBBS and serve society as a doctor. However, a life-transforming encounter with a revered saint ignited in him a deep spiritual calling. This path led him to receive initiation into higher knowledge from Sadgurudev Shri Satpal Ji Maharaj, which inspired him to embrace renunciation and dedicate decades to sharing spiritual wisdom.His unceasing quest for the Supreme Truth culminated in direct realization through the infinite grace of the Supreme Father God. Empowered with this divine command, he founded the Amarbelanand Foundation, wholly committed to delivering free, compassionate, and divinely guided services in Healthcare, Education, and Welfare. Beyond social service, the foundation carries the higher mission of offering spiritual guidance, reconnecting humanity with eternal truth, liberation, and divine love — fulfilling the will of the Supreme.

Our Activities

Free Holistic & Hitech Hospitals

We are establishing world-class, completely free hospitals offering not just modern but integrative healing-combining physical core with spiritual well-being. Every patient is seen as a reflection of the Supreme Divine.

Holistic & Value-Based Education

Education with soul-nurturing young minds in science, spirituality, compassion, and purpose. Our schools and learning centres will offer free, holistic, value-rooted education to all, from early childhood to advance research.

Global Welfare Centres

From food, clothing and shelter to counselling, rehabilitation, and livelihood-our welfare centres will support the needy with dignity and divine love, ensuring no soul is left behind as well as free community centres for socio-cultural activities.

Yoga & Divine Realization Programs

We offer global programs and retreats guiding seekers towards direct inner connection with the Supreme Vibration (Para-Brahma) through silent Rajayoga Vidya Method, vibration awareness, and divine inspiration-free of any charge.

Divine Media & Motivational Films

Creating spiritualy uplifting films, documentaries and digital media that inspire transformation, unity and love-bringing light to hearts and mind across the world.
